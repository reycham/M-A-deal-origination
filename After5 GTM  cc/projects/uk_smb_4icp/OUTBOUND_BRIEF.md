## How the copy pipeline will work

We're not writing 1,862 unique emails. The flow:

```
[Templates you write]  +  [AI-generated first liner per row]  →  [Final personalised email]
        12 total                       1,862 unique                 sent via SmartLead
```

### What you write (the morning task)

1. **Brand fundamentals** — one paragraph on what After5 Digital does + the strongest social-proof line we have. Drives the body of every sequence.

2. **4 ICP value-prop one-liners** — why a real estate agency vs a mortgage broker vs a dealership vs a recruitment firm specifically should care. These slot into the body as `{{icp_value_prop}}`.

3. **3 email skeletons** (Sequence A / B / C) — each is a subject line + body. The body has two placeholders:
   - `{{ai_first_liner}}` — 1–2 sentences, machine-written per recipient using their description + signals
   - `{{icp_value_prop}}` — the one-liner from step 2, picked by ICP

4. **3 LinkedIn DM equivalents** — same structure, shorter (LinkedIn DM character limits).

5. **CTA preference** — meeting? soft "interested?" reply? loom open? (Soft asks tend to outperform on UK SMB cold.)

That's **~15 short pieces of copy total**. Not 1,862. The AI does the per-row personalisation work.

### How the AI personalisation works

For each row, a script will call Claude with a prompt roughly like:

> "Company: [Description from CSV]. They run [X Meta ads, Y Google ads] and get [Z monthly visits] from [country]. Their decision-maker is [title]. Write a 2-sentence opener for a cold email that references something specific about their business and bridges naturally to {our offer}. Casual, not salesy. No emojis."

The model output goes into a new column `ai_first_liner` next to the row. Cost: ~$25 across all 1,862 rows.

**For Sequence C (low-signal rows)**, we drop the ad/traffic part of the prompt and rely on the company description only.

### Sequence assignment

Each row gets a `sequence` column based on tier:

| Tier | Sequence | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Hot + top half of Warm | **A** — full personalisation, 4-touch (Email + LinkedIn) over 14 days | Heavy signals warrant heavy investment |
| Bottom half of Warm + Cool | **B** — description-only personalisation, 3-touch over 10 days | Less signal density, lighter touch |
| Cold | **C** — ICP-templated, 2-touch | Volume play |

That's the proposed mapping — happy to adjust.

### SmartLead integration

The final output is one CSV per `(sequence × icp × persona)` pair, ready to upload to a SmartLead campaign. SmartLead substitutes `{{ai_first_liner}}` and `{{icp_value_prop}}` at send time. Twelve campaigns total.
